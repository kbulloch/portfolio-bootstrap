Education

I am a graduate of Epicodus, a full immersion code bootcamp in Portland, OR. Pair programming is the focus all day, every day at Epicodus. Pairing strengthens and challenges your knowledge base, and also fosters great communication skills. My technical education was forged around test driven development and object oriented programming.

Following Epicodus I had a two month internship at Copious where I helped develop a suite of automated tests for a large ecommerce site. I learned a lot about the backend of the Magento ecommerce CMS. I wrote backend functional tests with PHPSpec and front end web tests with Behat for a few dozen different use cases. I also manually tested and documented each test case for future engineers to reference.

Epifoodus

Epifoodus, AKA the Hangry App, was developed as a team project at Epicodus for those that are too hungry to decide where to eat for lunch. The app gives two random options for a place to eat nearby to Epicodus, and the option to display new choices. Users can create accounts to track what they liked and disliked. This app was built in PHP using Silex, Twig and PostgreSQL.
